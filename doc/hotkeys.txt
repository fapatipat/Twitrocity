Control+T: Tweet
Control+R: Reply
Control+Shift+R: Reply all.
Control+shift+T: retweet
control+shift+q: Quote
control+shift+U: View user.
control+shift+V: View tweet.
control+enter: Play audio
shift+enter: Pause audio.
control+shift+enter: Stop audio.
f5/f6: Adjust audio volume
control up/down: Change sound volume.
Enter: Open URL.
On mac, substitute control with command.
